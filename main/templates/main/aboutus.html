About Us

FlipEazy is created by people who felt there was a gap for all the Online Business enthusiasts who wanted to Buy & Sell low-end websites ranging from anywhere between $50-$1000. We wanted to fill that gap through FlipEazy which aims to be a reliable marketplace to Buy & Sell Quality Low-end websites.


We have an efficient process setup right from vetting the website, doing our due diligence to listing the website on the marketplace. We assure you of the quality and at the same time we charge a fixed 10% commission, which is pretty low when compared to our competitors.
