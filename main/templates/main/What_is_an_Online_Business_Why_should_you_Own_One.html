What is an Online Business? Why should you Own One?

More and More people are pivoting towards newer Business Opportunities and one of them being “Online Business”. Most of the Millenials are Looking to build their “Side Hustle” apart from their usual 9-5 Job.

Agreed ?

But, Guess What?

Online Business can actually be their side hustle, in fact with the right kind of knowledge and persistence they have the opportunity to scale and turn this into a Full-time Job.

But Here’s the thing,

These Businesses are not some kind of “Get Rich quick scheme”. It Involves Hard and smart work like any other Business for that matter. Moreover, It requires the right set of knowledge and skills to run an Online Business.

So We are going to delve into the topic of What exactly is an Online Business and Why should you Own One?
Online Businesses are Businesses that help people earn money through the Internet.


It is much easier to run an online business when compared to your traditional Brick and mortar Businesses, the reason being it is not capital intensive and ROI is crazy for the amount of time you put in.

Also, it is in the hands of the Owner to decide on what kind of business model can actually suit them based on their Interests and their area of Expertise.
Now let us take a look at Different Online Business models that you can cash in on:

GOOGLE ADSENSE:

We’ve all come across this platform and it is one of the prominent ways to earn money online. When you integrate this platform on your blog and you drive traffic to your content site and when the people who read your blog start clicking on the ads, Google pays you a fixed amount. If your content game is on point, then the ROI is crazy.

AMAZON AFFILAITE :

If you are a newbie and you’ve just entered the online business world, we recommend that you start off with an Amazon affiliate. Since Amazon is a trusted brand, the conversion rate is much higher especially when you are reviewing various Products. At the same time, there are various platforms like amazon wherein you can sign-up as an affiliate and build a website around those products. But the conversion rate might not be high when compared to Amazon, unless and until the product or the niche you are in is actually solving a particular problem.

DROPSHIPPING :

Dropshipping has been attracting a lot of eyeballs as it does not involve spending money on product research, warehousing, etc. All you have to do is to set up that perfect sales funnel so that the manufacturer can directly ship it to the customer. The profit margins are lean when compared to your traditional E-commerce Business, but if you are a digital marketing expert, these kinds of businesses are highly scalable. Most of the dropshipping websites are actually hosted on Shopify.

INFO PRODUCTS :

If you’ve been on the internet for a decent amount of time then you must have come across various websites where someone is selling an Ebook On weight loss or on How to earn money online etc. You need not limit yourself to only these topics; it can range from dating advice to financial guidance to teaching How to communicate and stuff like these which are actually Informative. Once the info product is ready and people are buying, you can actually scale it to the next level.

E-COMMERCE :

You all must be aware of how E-commerce works. This exactly works like a retail store but digitally, wherein you buy a product, find a warehouse where you can store and ship it to the customer using various internet marketing strategies,
There are many more Business models but these are the top 5 Models if you are just starting out and looking to gain experience and at the same time experiment with different business models.
However, it is important for you to have a little knowledge of how to go about these Businesses, otherwise, it can get a little messy.
Now, let us talk about,

Why Should You Own an Online Business?


PASSIVE INCOME :

What if you had a chance to put your business on Auto-pilot mode and all you have to do is keep a track of how much money is in the bank? Sounds fun, right? That’s what Online business can do, but trust me it’s not a cakewalk to get there. It requires a bit of hard work in the initial stages.
It actually gives you that extra time for you to be indulged in other activities, at the same time you can also look to diversify your portfolio of websites or you Have an opportunity to diversify your income streams as well.

FLEXIBILITY :

Every Business has to be flexible and not fixed to one particular income stream, as the saying goes,” Do not put all your eggs in one basket”, this holds true when you are running an Online Business.
When you know your website is getting traction or not making enough money, then it is really easy for the owner to shift from one business model to another without burning his/her pockets, unlike a traditional Brick and mortar Business.

MINIMUM CAPITAL :

The cost required for you to start an Online business is the bare minimum when compared to other businesses. All you require is a Domain and hosting. If you are planning to Outsource everything, then it might cost you a bit. But keep in mind it is worth taking that leap of faith and experiment with the Online business models, You will learn.

SCALABILITY :

In the Online Business world there is no dead-end. There is always room for scaling the existing website. For example, if you're currently making $300, then there is always a possibility for you to scale it to $500 or $700 by tweaking some things like increasing the number of backlinks, ranking for high- competitive keywords, fixing the page speed which in turn might lead to an increase in the conversion rate and the most important thing is to keep Updating the existing content and at the same writing new content consistently covering all the topics in your niche.

PROFITBALE EXIT :

Online business can give you high returns if you are planning to sell it because as per the industry norms a profitable online business can be flipped for almost 3x the annual revenue. For example: If you are running a blog that is monetized through Google Adsense and is making around $300/month i.e. 3600/year and boom and your website is valued $10,800. But there are a lot of factors to be considered before valuing the website.


CONCLUSION :

You might be thinking which model is right fit for you?

Well,

All the business models explained above have their own Pros and Cons of running it and all of these models can be scaled to Six-figure businesses with the right set of skills.

Our suggestion would be to start whatever you are interested in, experiment with different techniques, get your hands dirty, it might be a little intimidating in the beginning, but as time passes you will understand where you exactly stand.

Online business is the new normal and we hope you get the most out of it.
