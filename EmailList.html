How to create an E-mail list to grow your existing Business ?

Are you still assuming creating an E-mail list is a mere waste of time?

Well, you are wrong my friend!

If at all you are planning to curate a marketing strategy for your digital asset, Creating an E-mail list should on top of your mind, Why?

Because it can generate chunks of revenue and at the same increase the valuation of your business.

If at all you’ve tried and tested creating an e-mail list and were not able to succeed, then you have come to the right place. We will sort you out with the right information.

Make sure you stick till the end!

Let us dive into the topic. First things first,

Are you using a Personal or a Business E-mail ID?

This might sound completely unnecessary to a newbie, but the open rate of your mail by the receiver depends on how you identify yourself in the mail.

So rather than sending an email from id’s like name@gmail.com, it is better to stick to business id’s like name@yourbusiness.com and this kind of brings in that trust factor and chances of opening the mail by the other person is much higher.

How to create a Business E-mail?

Though there are tons of service providers for this, the most preferable is Gmail as it is very easy to use and most of them are familiar with this.

G-suite will walk you through all the necessary steps when you go to their page. You will need access to your DNS records and depending on the hosting provider it will guide you on verification of your domain.

Now, we have set up the Business e-mail id, what next?

Automation or Auto-responder

Autoresponder reduces the work for you by automating the e-mail process and helps in sending mails in larger volume all at once.

This kind of tool helps in sequencing the emails for you like:

play_arrow Sending a welcome e-mail

play_arrowGiveaways

play_arrowWebinar Links

All these can mails can be sent one after the other.

Once the mail id is entered by the user, the autoresponder starts sequencing the e-mails for you.

The work required from your side is to draft the e-mails, it can be personalized depending on your audience or you can have one consistent draft to each of your subscribers.

There are a lot of customizations you can always do like sending discount offers to your subscribers on their birthdays or festivals or on national holidays etc.

Till now we’ve set up a business mail and sequenced our mails using Autoresponder software.

How to build an E-mail list?

Just doing the above 2 tasks is not sufficient, the money is in the list, so it is important to build a list of your targeted audience and how can you do that?

Well,

It actually works great when you follow a Give and take policy, meaning when a person gives out his e-mail id he is expecting something in return right? We all do. So the way to do this is to set up a lead magnet.

What is a Lead Magnet?

A lead magnet is something that you offer to your audience which is free and valuable in order to collect the necessary contact details. It is a great marketing strategy to build your e-mail list.

A customer is willing to give out his e-mail id when he is getting something in return. PDFs and E-books work wonders especially when they are really informative.

It is also important to create a giveaway that is going to be evergreen and people can refer to it repeatedly.

You need not spend time on writing PDF’s and E-books unless and until you are someone who is an expert in the niche or you are a great content writer. You can always outsource the work by hiring someone from Upwork.

It is a given fact that in order to create a list, You need traffic, it can be either paid traffic, organic traffic, or through social media. You do not want people to be on your e-mail list who are not your audience, so ensure that when you are getting paid traffic always ensure you are targeting the right set of audiences.

Now, let us talk about

Placement of the form

This is something that varies from site to site and it is recommended that you do some kind of A/B testing and see what kind of placement works. It can be

play_arrow Pop-up form

play_arrowIn-article forms

play_arrowLanding pages

play_arrowBanner Ads

The form placement shouldn’t annoy the user by giving them a bad user experience and you should always make sure the forms do not distract your visitors so much that they exit the website.

Try to keep it simple and place your form in the articles or on the homepage. Keep testing and you will understand what works best for you.

If you are planning to use Pop-up forms, ensure that it is designed in such a way that it only pops up if a user has spent a decent amount of time on the website.

Advantages of Building an E-mail list.

We spoke about the process of building an e-mail list.

Now, we will talk about how this process can be an added advantage to you as a business owner.

You should always consider E-mail marketing as an investment that will yield returns because having a list of people who have subscribed to your newsletter will eventually click on your new offerings directly through the link on the mail.

There is no need for visitors to navigate to that page. This also acts as a channel for driving that traffic who are mostly people that follow the same niche you are in

If at all you plan on selling your business to someone else, Creating an E-mail list will always fetch you a higher valuation. The future buyer will gain confidence that the site is not solely dependent on one single channel for driving traffic.

CONCLUSION :

Building an e-mail list wouldn’t be a problem if you actually take some time and do some research on what your audience likes and based on your findings you can offer them an e-book or a podcast that is valuable to them and at the same time free of cost.

As they say “Content is king and engagement is queen”. So engagement is key when you are planning to build an e-mail list.
